Interest is on dependencies unlike previous models.

- **Independence** assumption and **memorylessness**
- Whatever happened provides no information on what will

Time $Y_k$ of the kth success is equal to $T_1 + T_2 + \dots + T_k$ of k independent geometric random variables.

It has the following properties:
$P_{Y_K}(t)={t-1 \choose k-1}p^k(1-p)^{t-k}$ for $t \ge k$

$\mathbb{E}[Y_k]=\frac{k}{p}$
$var(Y_k)=\frac{k(1-p)}{p^2}$

**Markov**
$P(X \ge a)\le \frac{\mathbb{E}[X]}{a}$
$P(|X-\mu|\ge c) \le \frac{\sigma^2}{c^2}$
