Instead of defining $y$ in terms of $x$ or $x$ in terms of $y$, we can define both $x$ and $y$ in terms of a third variable called a parameter as follows,
$$x=f(t) \qquad y=g(t)$$
The graph of all $(x, y)$ is called the *parametric curve*.
