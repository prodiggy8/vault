CRISPR techj allows scientist to make changes to DNA in cells.

Came about through a basic research project that wanted to find how bacteria fight viral infections.

Bacteria has adaptive immune system called CRISPR that allows them to detect viral DNA and destroy it

Cas9 is part of CRISPR, seeks out cut and degrade viral DNA. Can be harnessed as a genetic engineering technology, inserting or deleting bits of DNA into cells.

Has been used to change DNA in cells of mice and monkeys, as well as other organisms. Chinese scietists did it in hu
