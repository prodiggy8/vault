**Cones** = colors, focus, 3 types (red, green, and blue)
**Rods** = dim light, movement

**Homunculus** exhibit topographic map of touch

Visible light: animals have it different, even different colors (number of cones)

**Retina:** light processing with photoreceptors

**Cornea**: outer covering
**Lens**: focus light
**Iris**: opens/close to let light in

**Blindspot** in the middle where the optic nerve is

1. Photoreceptors
2. Bipolar cells
3. Retinal Ganglial Cells (RGC): basic light processing, connected to optic nerve
4. Horizontal cells: connect receptors to bipolar
5. Amacrine cells: connects bipolar to RGC
